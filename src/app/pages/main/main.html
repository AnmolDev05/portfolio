About Me

I’m Anmol Sharma, a passionate Front-End Developer with over 4 years of experience crafting dynamic, responsive, and user-friendly web applications. When I’m not building interfaces, you’ll find me exploring new tech trends, fine-tuning side projects, or enjoying a good coffee while sketching out my next idea.
